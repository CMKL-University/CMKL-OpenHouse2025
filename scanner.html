📷 Camera Troubleshooting

📱 Android Devices

1. Grant Permission
Tap "Allow" when browser asks for camera access
2. Check Settings
Settings → Apps → Chrome → Permissions → Camera → Allow
3. Browser Issues
Clear browser cache or try Chrome/Firefox
4. HTTPS Required
Make sure URL starts with "https://"

🔧 General Issues

Camera Blocked?
Click the camera icon 📷 in your address bar
Camera In Use?
Close other camera apps and refresh page
Still Not Working?
Try incognito mode or different browser
